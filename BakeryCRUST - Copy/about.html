About Us

Golden Crust Bakery is a family-owned business founded in 2015 in Durban, South Africa. It began in a home kitchen, baking for friends and neighbors. As demand grew, a small shop was opened. Today, the bakery is known for its fresh breads, cakes, and pastries—blending traditional recipes with modern flavors. It remains a local favorite.

Our mission is to bring delicious baked goods to everyone in our community.
